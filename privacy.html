Privacy Policy

At Ludus, your privacy is important. By using our Discord bot, you consent to the practices described below. If you do not agree, please discontinue use of Ludus.

1. Information Collection

Ludus collects minimal information necessary for bot functionality, including:

  • Command usage logs to improve bot performance and experience.
  • Basic server and user identifiers (e.g., user ID, guild ID) for command execution and feature management.
  • Optional data provided by users, such as custom messages or preferences.

2. Use of Information

  • To operate the bot’s features effectively.
  • To provide analytics and improve services.
  • To ensure compliance with our Terms of Service and Discord’s guidelines.

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information. Ludus may share information only in the following cases:

  • To comply with legal obligations or enforce our Terms of Service.
  • To prevent abuse or protect other users from harm.
  • To trusted third-party service providers who assist with bot functionality (e.g., hosting providers) while ensuring data protection.

4. Data Retention

Data is retained only as long as necessary to provide bot functionality. Usage logs may be anonymized or deleted after a period of time.

5. Security

We implement reasonable security measures to protect your data. However, we cannot guarantee absolute security for any information transmitted through Discord or our bot services.

6. Third-Party Links

Ludus may contain links to third-party services or websites. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of external services.

7. User Rights

  • You may request deletion of any user-specific data collected by Ludus.
  • Discontinuing use of the bot will stop further data collection.

8. Updates to Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Continued use of Ludus after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

9. Contact

Questions or concerns about privacy can be addressed via our support server: Psyvrse Discord.
